About The Park
Abilene’s Best Monthly RV Community

Buck Creek RV Park is a well-maintained monthly community located just off I-20 in Abilene, Texas — close to Hendrick Medical Center, restaurants, shopping, and all major highways.

Whether you’re a travel nurse on assignment, a contractor working the area, or looking for long-term RV living, Buck Creek Abilene is your home base in West Texas. We are currently at capacity for RV sites. Tiny homes are available — join the waitlist below.

Monthly stays only. Water and sewer included. Electric billed separately by meter.
